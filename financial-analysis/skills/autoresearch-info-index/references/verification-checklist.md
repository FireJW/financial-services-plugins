# Info Index Verification Checklist

Before keeping a candidate, check:

- all mentions of "today", "just now", or "latest" are converted into absolute dates
- the note distinguishes what one party said from what other parties confirmed
- every conclusion about credibility is tied to source evidence
- the note says what is unknown, not only what is known
- market significance is separated from factual event confirmation
- the output exposes a credibility snapshot instead of hiding uncertainty inside prose
- the output clearly separates `core_verdict` from `live_tape`
- a shadow signal did not raise the main confidence level by itself
- evidence older than 24 hours is marked as background unless reactivated
- blocked high-value sources are visible in `sources_blocked`
- crisis-mode vessel rows use `last public location` and ETA ranges, not fake precision
