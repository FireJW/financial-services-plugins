# Jenny Wen Pattern

Use this as a pattern, not a verbatim source.

## What the Jenny Wen case supports

- teams still read research and feedback directly
- AI helps compress notes, calls, and messy feedback into a usable structure
- human judgment still turns evidence into design principles and priorities
- releasing early can be correct if the team visibly listens and iterates
- users must feel heard, not just shipped to

## What the Jenny Wen case does not fully prove

- one fixed canonical SOP
- one fixed weekly artifact format as a direct quote
- a claim that AI replaced research or product judgment

## Best-use Pattern

Apply this case when the user asks for:

- "how does a design or product team use AI to digest feedback"
- "turn this interview into a workflow"
- "design me a weekly customer-feedback synthesis loop"
- "what is the minimal SOP behind this leader's feedback process"
