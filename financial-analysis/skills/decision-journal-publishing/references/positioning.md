# Positioning

## Core Thesis

The account should not be a "finance hot-news account", an "AI tutorial
account", or a "prediction personality". It should be an evidence-layered
decision journal with one commercial center and one retail-safe wrapper.

Recommended positioning:

`面向需要反复处理事件冲击的人，把 24 小时噪音整理成可验证的研判，并把判断流程沉淀成可复用模板。`

Short version:

`把消息变成判断，把判断变成模板。`

## Commercial Center

Keep one center of gravity:

1. professional investors
2. trading and research users
3. operators who must turn events into decisions or briefings

Retail expansion is allowed, but it is not the center of the business.

## Audience Stack

### Layer 1: Core paid users

- buy-side research
- trading
- asset allocation
- advisory and IR operators
- anyone who must repeatedly turn events into decisions and communication

### Layer 2: Adjacent paid users

- high-participation retail investors
- serious self-directed investors
- users who value process, risk framing, and post-mortem discipline

### Layer 3: Free traffic users

- casual retail readers
- hot-topic followers
- users who want excitement more than process

Do not optimize the business for layer 3.

## What Expanding to Retail Should Mean

Expand to retail only by adding a retail-safe wrapper:

1. simpler language
2. clearer scenario framing
3. stronger risk and invalidation cues
4. more explicit "how to read this event" guidance

Do not expand by shifting to:

1. exact targets
2. certainty language
3. "神预测" branding
4. daily tipster behavior
5. a separate low-end business that redefines the whole account

## Funnel Split

The same research engine should support two wrappers:

### Pro wrapper

- language: decision memo
- value: structure, repeatability, briefing efficiency
- CTA: full template pack, update rights, sample library

### Retail wrapper

- language: event reading and risk framing
- value: how to read a sudden event without acting on noise
- CTA: lite template, event-reading skeleton, then the same core pack

Do not build two unrelated products for these two audiences in the first month.

## Promise

Always promise:

1. faster understanding
2. clearer uncertainty handling
3. reusable decision structure
4. public review of outcomes

## 10W+ Benchmark Warning

Do not use `10W+` as the account's north star.

Reason:

1. a 10W+ article proves that a topic escaped the niche
2. it does not prove that the readers are buyers
3. the highest-value finance readers often live in smaller but denser content

Use two lenses instead:

1. `public break-out fit`
2. `buyer-density fit`

The positioning should optimize for the second while still learning from the first.

## Anti-Promise

Never promise:

1. guaranteed hit rate
2. exact market prophecy
3. guaranteed returns
4. one-click stock recommendations
5. certainty where evidence is weak

## Reframe "Prediction"

Bad framing:

- "我提前预言了"
- "下一只翻倍股"
- "这次我敢打包票"

Good framing:

- `本周预判板`
- `情景树`
- `触发条件`
- `失效条件`
- `复盘记分牌`

The account should earn loyalty through transparent calibration, not mystical
certainty.

## Recommended Voice

- calm
- traceable
- non-theatrical
- specific about dates, windows, and conditions
- closer to a risk memo than a hype post
