Prompt 模板 + 3 个视觉方向变体

目标模型：GPT-4o Image Generation API（images/edits endpoint）· 输出 1242×1660px · 无文字

主 Prompt 模板结构

GPT-4o Image Gen — 主模板（5 段式）
[1. 媒介与画幅] Vertical editorial photograph, 3:4 aspect ratio, shot on photojournalism camera. [2. 主体与场景 — 从参考图提取] {根据参考新闻图描述主体} 例: A wide-angle view of oil tankers anchored in the Strait of Hormuz at dusk, warm amber light reflecting off calm water, distant naval vessels on the horizon. [3. 构图与安全区] Compose the main subject in the upper 65-70% of the frame. The bottom 30% should have lower visual density — soft gradient, out-of-focus foreground, or atmospheric haze — to serve as a text-safe zone for overlay. Do NOT leave the bottom blank or empty; maintain visual continuity. [4. 气质与色调 — 从参考图提取] {根据参考图的视觉气质} 例: Muted photojournalistic color grading, desaturated warm tones, natural available light, slight grain. The mood is tense and watchful, not dramatic or cinematic. [5. 禁止项（内嵌 negative）] Do NOT include any text, titles, captions, watermarks, logos, UI elements, or typographic overlays in the image. Do NOT render the image as a poster, magazine cover, or advertisement layout. No stock photo aesthetics, no HDR over-processing, no artificial lens flare.

3 个视觉方向变体

变体 A — 纪实摄影感

新闻现场 · 真实质感

Vertical editorial photograph, 3:4 aspect ratio, photojournalism style. Wide-angle view of oil tankers anchored near the Strait of Hormuz at golden hour. Warm amber light reflects off still water. A distant naval patrol vessel sits on the horizon. Slight heat haze distorts the far shore. Main subject fills the upper 65% of the frame. The bottom 30% transitions into soft out-of-focus water surface with gentle light reflections — visually continuous but low-density for text overlay. Muted photojournalistic color grading: desaturated warm tones, natural available light, subtle film grain. The mood is tense, watchful, documentary. NOT dramatic, NOT cinematic, NOT heroic. No text, no titles, no captions, no watermarks, no logos, no UI elements. Not a poster. Not a magazine cover. Not an advertisement. No stock photo look. No HDR. No artificial lens flare. No oversaturated colors.

变体 B — 数据可视化 / 信息图感

地图 · 区域标注 · 地缘信息

Vertical editorial infographic-style photograph, 3:4 aspect ratio. Aerial satellite-perspective view of the Persian Gulf and Strait of Hormuz. Stylized like a military situation room display — dark navy background, landmasses in muted olive and sand tones, water in deep teal. Subtle grid lines suggest a tactical map. A few abstract route lines (warm amber) trace shipping lanes through the strait. Main geographic detail concentrated in the upper 65%. The bottom 30% fades into deep ocean tone — dark, uniform, low-information-density area suitable for text overlay. Color palette: dark navy, deep teal, muted sand, warm amber accents. Restrained and serious. Think intelligence briefing, not infographic poster. Subtle paper texture. No text, no labels, no country names, no legends, no annotations, no watermarks. Not a PowerPoint slide. Not a textbook map. No bright primary colors. No cartoon styling. No 3D globe rendering.

变体 C — 抽象隐喻感

象征性视觉 · 情绪氛围

Vertical editorial art photograph, 3:4 aspect ratio, fine art documentary style. A single chess piece (a dark king) standing on a reflective surface that resembles an oil slick — iridescent dark tones of petroleum blue and amber. The piece casts a long dramatic shadow. Behind it, a blurred horizon line suggests an uncertain boundary between sea and sky. The composition evokes strategic tension and high-stakes calculation. The chess piece occupies the upper-center 60% of the frame. The reflective surface extends into the bottom 30%, becoming progressively darker and more uniform — a natural text-safe zone. Color palette: deep petroleum blue, dark amber, near-black reflections, minimal warm highlights. Mood: ominous, contemplative, strategic. Shot with shallow depth of field, the background is intentionally abstract. No text, no titles, no symbols, no icons, no watermarks. Not a movie poster. Not a book cover. Not a stock photo. No chess board — only the single piece on the reflective surface. No bright colors. No fantasy lighting.

Negative Prompt / 禁止元素（通用）

所有变体共用 — 复制到 system prompt 或 negative 字段
## 绝对禁止 - Any text, typography, titles, captions, labels, watermarks, or logos - Any UI elements, buttons, frames, or decorative borders - Poster layout, magazine cover layout, advertisement composition - Stock photo aesthetics (over-lit, over-posed, generic smile) - HDR over-processing, artificial lens flare, chromatic aberration effects - Oversaturated or neon colors - 3D rendering, CGI look, video game aesthetic - Cartoon, illustration, or anime style - Multiple exposure or collage composition ## 气质禁止（避免 AI slop） - "Epic" or "cinematic" dramatic lighting (→ 用 "available light" 替代) - Symmetrical centered composition (→ 用 "slightly off-center" 替代) - Perfect skin / perfect weather / perfect everything (→ 加 "imperfect", "lived-in") - Hyper-detailed micro-textures (→ 用 "natural detail level" 替代) - "Award-winning photograph" (→ 这个词是 AI slop 的万恶之源，永远不要用) ## 安全区禁止 - Do not place important subjects in the bottom 30% of the frame - Do not leave the bottom 30% completely blank or white - The bottom zone must have visual continuity but low information density
